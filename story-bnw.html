You swallow the soma. A wave of artificial calm washes over you. Lenina laughs and pulls you to a Solidarity Service, where Alphas and Betas chant and dance in a ritualized orgy. For the first time, you feel... happy.

Days later, Director Mustapha Mond promotes you for your "admirable conformity." You’re given access to the Hatchery and Conditioning Centre, where rows of tiny Epsilons are decanted for manual labor.
