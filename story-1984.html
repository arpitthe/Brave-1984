You push the soma away. Lenina frowns but says nothing. That night, a woman slips you a note: "Meet me in the Prole District. —Julia".

In a crumbling apartment, Julia introduces you to John the Savage, a man raised outside the World State. He shows you a forbidden book: Shakespeare's Collected Works. "You’re not a machine," he says. "You can feel."
